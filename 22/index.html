Intersections

Notes

Genuary 2023 day 4 prompt is intersections. This is just a series of bezier curves with some randomness being drawn onto the canvas, with normal distribution applied to the positions.

Created with p5.js, a JavaScript library for creative coding.

made by Nick Ang (IG) on 4 Jan 2023 in the living room of an apartment in the south of Spain as part of the Everydays project.
