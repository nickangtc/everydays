Endless splash (Gaussian 2)

Notes

It occurred to me while I was driving today that paint thrown onto a wall is a kind of Gaussian distribution. Most of the paint will be in the center and the rest are spread out as part of the splash.

3 for loops that generate 3 concentric circles of Gaussian-distributed oval shapes (ellipses) of random heights and widths = a paintbrush splash on the wall?

made by Nick Ang (IG) on 28 Dec 2022 as part of Everydays project.
