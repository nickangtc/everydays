Secret (asemic writing)

notes

Today's Gennuary prompt was: asemic writing. Yet another thing I had no idea about before the prompt! Fun!

My first thought was that I could use a Perlin walker to draw out something on the canvas, then destroy it, and add a new one, and destroy that, and repeat to create a feeling of an invisible hand writing on the canvas trying to scribble something meaningful to you and I. Will look so mysterious!
